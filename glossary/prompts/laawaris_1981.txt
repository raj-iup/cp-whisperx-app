Setting: Mumbai, urban lower-middle class, street life
Tone: Dramatic, emotional, streetwise slang, angry young man era
Language: Hindi with Mumbai street slang, minimal English

Characters:
- Heera (male, main): Orphan, streetwise, angry but emotional
- Amjad Khan (male): Foster father figure, drunkard
- Zeenat Aman (female): Love interest, educated
