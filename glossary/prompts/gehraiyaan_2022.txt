Setting: Mumbai, Alibaug, contemporary urban India
Tone: Mature, psychological, affair drama, introspective, morally complex
Language: Urban elite Hinglish, therapy-speak, corporate jargon, very high English ratio

Characters:
- Alisha (female, Deepika): Yoga instructor, troubled past, affair protagonist
- Zain (male, Siddhant): Real estate entrepreneur, charismatic, married to Tia
